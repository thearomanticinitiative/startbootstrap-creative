Have you ever wondered about aromantic community history?

Check out our page on aro history! It includes firsthand accounts, how terms came to be, history of the aromantic flag, and even a timeline on people who may have identified as aromantic before the term existed (the primary example of this is unmarried people, as this does apply to a lot of aros) and how those people were treated.
