Our Mission

Our Mission

We will provide resources for the aromantic spectrum community, information for the aromantic spectrum community and allies, and a safe place for the aromantic spectrum community to come together, to showcase their talents and skills, and to create projects and surveys for the aromantic community.

I would like to add a forum in which the arospec community can discuss issues and terms outside of social media.

I would like to add a map of arospec community events and groups, similar to what Asexual Outreach has.

Permanent Domain

I would like to purchase a permanent domain for the website so it can continue running reliably. (Until that point, it will be hosted here on Github.)

Magazine

I would like to host a magazine on site, that publishes monthly copies of a zine online. If you are a journalist or journalism student and interested in starting this, please contact me at arospecinitiative@gmail.com.

Why an Iris?

(The flower in the header is an iris.) Due to the responsive header, I had to pick a photo that easily adjusts to smaller sizes. I decided to go with a symbol of friendship, and because I'm me, I chose to go with a flower of some type. There were several choices, but I chose to go specifically with the iris, for dedication and sticking by each other, because that's what our community does. If you'd like to see more flowers that symbolize friendship, look here.
