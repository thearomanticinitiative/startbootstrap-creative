What is Aromanticism?

All about aromanticism.

What is the definiton of aromanaticism?

Aromanticism is defined as not feeling romantic attraction, or feeling it rarely, or under only specific circumstances. The aromantic spectrum includes identities like gray-aromantic, demiromantic, lithromantic, and quoiromantic.

Aromanticism is not...

Hating romance

Not wanting a relationship

Not wanting to get married

Not wanting kids

Not being able to get a date

Asexuality (feeling sexual attraction never, rarely, or under specific circumstances)

Aromanticism is simply feeling rare - no romantic attraction.
