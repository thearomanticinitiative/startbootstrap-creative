What is Aromanticism?

What is aromanaticism?

Aromanticism is defined as not feeling romantic attraction.The aromantic spectrum includes identities like gray-aromantic, demiromantic, lithromantic, and quoiromantic-identities that indicate feeling romantic attraction rarely or under certain circumstances.

Stereotypes

Aromanticism is not being heartless, hating romance, not wanting a relationship/to get married, not wanting kids, or not being able to get a date. It is also not asexuality, which is not feeling sexual attraction. These are all common stereotypes, but many of them depend on the person.

Symbols

The aromantic flag, pictured at right, is one of the symbols of the aromantic community. Another less used symbol is a white ring on the middle finger, worn as a symbol of personal pride- similar to the black ring asexual people wear on the right middle finger.
