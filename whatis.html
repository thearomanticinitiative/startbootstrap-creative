What is Aromanticism?

What is aromanaticism?

Aromanticism is defined as not feeling romantic attraction.The aromantic spectrum includes identities like gray-aromantic, demiromantic, lithromantic, and quoiromantic-identities that indicate feeling romantic attraction rarely or under certain circumstances.

Aromanticism is not...

Hating romance

Not wanting a relationship

Not wanting to get married

Not wanting kids

Not being able to get a date

Asexuality (not feeling sexual attraction)

Aromanticism is simply feeling no romantic attraction. Some aros do fit the above stereotypes, but some do not- and thus it is rude to assume either way.

The Aromantic Flag

The aromantic flag is one of the symbols of the aromantic community, along with a white ring worn on the left middle finger. Both of these symbols reflect the connnection the aro community has with the ace community-the flags are very similar, with the shades of black, gray, and white being the same and the ring stemming from the "ace ring": a black ring worn on the right middle finger.
